Commercial Flood Restoration Across the Bay Area

Commercial flooding — from storms, burst mains, sprinkler failures, or rising water — is one of the most disruptive events a business can face. Large volumes of water spread fast across commercial floor plates, affecting multiple systems and tenants at once. Allied Restoration provides commercial flood restoration across the San Francisco Bay Area with the owned large-loss equipment fleet that big water events demand.

The Commercial Flood Response Sequence

  • Emergency water extraction — high-volume pumps and extractors remove standing water fast
  • Structural drying at scale — desiccant dehumidification and air movers dry large spaces
  • Temporary power — three-phase power runs equipment where building power is compromised
  • Content and document recovery — salvaging what can be saved from a flooded commercial space
  • Sewage and contamination handling — safely managing Category 3 floodwater
  • Reconstruction — returning the property to pre-loss condition

Why Speed Determines Cost

In a commercial flood, every hour matters. Water wicks into walls, subfloors, and building systems, and mold begins within 24-48 hours. The faster water is extracted and the structure dried, the less it costs and the sooner you reopen.
