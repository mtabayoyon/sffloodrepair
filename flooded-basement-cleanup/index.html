Flooded Basement Cleanup

A flooded basement is one of the most common — and most damaging — water events for Bay Area properties. Basements collect water from storms, sump pump failures, burst pipes, and rising groundwater, and because they are below grade, the water sits and soaks. Allied provides fast, professional flooded basement cleanup: extraction, drying, sanitizing, and restoration.

Our Flooded Basement Cleanup Process

  • Safety first — checking for electrical and contamination hazards before entry
  • Water extraction — pumping and extracting standing water
  • Removing damaged materials — soaked carpet, drywall, and belongings as needed
  • Structural drying — drying the space thoroughly to prevent mold
  • Sanitizing — especially important for contaminated or sewage-tainted water
  • Restoration — repairing and rebuilding the basement

Why Basements Need Special Attention

Basements are prone to mold because they are dark, humid, and slow to dry — and floodwater there is often contaminated (Category 2 or 3). Improper cleanup leaves moisture in the structure and belongings, guaranteeing mold. Professional cleanup dries the space to standard and addresses contamination safely.
