Mold Remediation in San Francisco

It is a common mistake to assume mold only affects basements or attics. The presence of moisture anywhere can cause spores to spread throughout the house, leading to more problems and threatening the health of those living in the home. Mold remediation is the only efficient way to deal with the problem and prevent further spread.

If you're searching for a home mold remediation service in San Francisco, Allied Restoration can provide the first and best line of defense in ridding your house of mold and creating a more allergen-free living area for you and your family. Call us today at (415) 529-5637.

Even Slight Moisture Can Cause Mold to Form

Mold requires very little moisture or dampness to grow. What's worse is that it spreads very easily — especially in homes and buildings that have HVAC units. A musty or persistent odor — similar to that of dirty socks — is usually a clear sign of an issue. A thorough and professional home mold remediation is required in all of these cases to minimize the spread and potential hazards associated with a mold outbreak.

Mold Poses a Serious Health Risk

Mold isn't just the cause of annoying odors or occasional sneezing. For allergy sufferers, especially those highly sensitive to mold spores, constant allergic reactions throughout the year are common. Reactions are even more dangerous to those who suffer from asthma — spores can trigger attacks that are longer lasting and more difficult to address. Mold has also been observed to contribute to the development of respiratory illnesses over time, especially in children.

Mold Spreads Easily Through a House

Any home, especially one with a central air and heating unit, can become susceptible to the spread of mold. Active mold sites frequently release spores which travel easily through the air, finding moist areas in bathroom or kitchen fixtures and creating new mold sites. Don't let mold take over your home. Call Allied Restoration at 415-529-5637, and start breathing easier today in a house free of mold, spores and moisture.

Our Mold Remediation Process

If there has been a prolonged moisture intrusion in your home, mold has likely begun to grow. In fact, mold grows within 72 hours after a water intrusion event. Allied Restoration Company's mold remediation process:

  • We test the suspected area for mold and perform air tests to locate traveling spores
  • We test for abnormally high moisture levels to identify the source
  • We consult with owners about the building history and any prior water damage
  • We deliver a written estimate for the remediation and any property repairs
  • We provide thorough remediation addressing all contaminated areas
  • We conduct a follow-up test to confirm all mold sites are clean
