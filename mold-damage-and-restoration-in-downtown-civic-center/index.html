Mold Damage and Restoration in Downtown/Civic Center

Downtown/Civic Center Mold Risk: San Francisco's Downtown and Civic Center district has high mold risk due to older commercial buildings, underground parking structures, and the area's dense fog exposure. Many buildings here were constructed before modern moisture barriers were standard.

Mold Damage and Restoration in Downtown/Civic Center

If portions of your Downtown/Civic Center neighborhood home have been exposed to moisture for a prolonged time, it is likely that mold growth has begun. Mold growth begins within 72 hours after moisture exposure. Along with damage to your home, mold can be dangerous to your health, compromising the quality of your life and those living in your home.

With our high-tech equipment, our experts at Allied Restoration Company will perform room-to-room mold inspections to find the source and identify any spread of mold spores. We will make sure the problem is addressed thoroughly and quickly. After cleaning the mold from your home and restoring your property, we will finish with a followup air quality test to be certain sure all sources of mold spores have been destroyed.

If you suspect that your Downtown/Civic Center home is contaminated by mold, contact Allied Restoration Company as soon as possible so our experts can determine the origin and extent of mold contamination and begin a restoration plan.
