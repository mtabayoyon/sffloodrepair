Common Water Damage Causes in Santa Clara

  • Burst and leaking pipes — aging supply lines and freeze events in Santa Clara County properties
  • Appliance failures — washing machines, dishwashers, water heaters, and refrigerator ice lines
  • Roof leaks — storm-driven rain infiltration through aging or damaged roof systems
  • HVAC failures — condensation line blockages and air handler pan overflows
  • Slab leaks — supply line failures beneath concrete slabs, often undetected for weeks
  • Sewage backup — drain blockages causing Category 3 water intrusion requiring biohazard protocols
