Structural Restoration

The Bay Area and Marin County are well known around the world as a region supporting some of the most historic and beautiful homes in the nation. It is also an area prone to fires, earthquakes, mud slides, sudden Pacific storms and senseless acts of vandalism. No matter when disaster strikes, we are ready to put things right.

Everyone deserves to have their property restored with meticulous care no matter whether you own a major corporation or a small family home. At any point during your insurance claim process, feel free to contact us to answer your questions about the restoration of your property.

When you come to us for honest advice, we thoroughly inspect the structural damage so we can tell you exactly what needs to be done. We deploy moisture meters, specially-built cameras, chemical analysis kits and a range of other professional hardware. These instruments go beyond the physical damage to test for collateral damage like mold, rot and structural weaknesses.

Our full assessment of the restoration requirements put you back on solid ground with a clear picture of the project scope.
