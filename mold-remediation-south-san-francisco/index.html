Common Mold Locations in South San Francisco Homes

  • Crawl spaces — ground moisture and limited ventilation create ideal mold conditions beneath South San Francisco homes
  • Attics — inadequate ventilation causes condensation that supports mold growth on roof sheathing
  • Behind bathroom walls — tile grout is surface mold; mold on drywall behind tiles is a more serious concern
  • Wall cavities after water damage — mold colonizes wet drywall paper within 24–72 hours
  • HVAC systems — cooling coils and condensate pans that develop mold distribute spores throughout the building
  • Basements and lower levels — foundation seepage and flooding create persistent moisture
