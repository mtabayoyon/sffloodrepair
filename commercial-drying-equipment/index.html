Industrial Drying Equipment for Restoration

Allied Restoration maintains a comprehensive fleet of industrial restoration equipment, allowing us to tackle jobs of any scale — from a single-family home with a burst pipe to a 300-unit commercial building with catastrophic flooding.

Our Equipment Fleet Includes

  • Desiccant dehumidifiers (up to 5,000 CFM)
  • Refrigerant dehumidifiers
  • High-velocity air movers
  • Negative air machines with HEPA filtration
  • Truck-mounted water extractors
  • Portable extraction units
  • Thermal imaging cameras
  • Professional moisture meters
  • Temporary power generators
  • Drying chambers

Temporary Power Services

When utility power is unavailable after a disaster, Allied Restoration provides temporary generator power to keep drying equipment running and maintain building systems during the restoration process. This is critical for preventing secondary damage and keeping the drying timeline on track.

5,000 CFM Desiccant Dehumidification

For large commercial jobs, severe water losses, or cold/humid conditions where refrigerant dehumidifiers are ineffective, we deploy desiccant dehumidifiers capable of processing up to 5,000 cubic feet of air per minute. These are essential for drying large structural assemblies, crawl spaces, and commercial spaces quickly and thoroughly.
