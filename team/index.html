⸻ Michael – Founder, Leader, and Chief Vision Wrangler Michael co-founded Allied Restoration Company with his dad back in 2002 — before TikTok, smart homes, or oat milk lattes. They started with one location in Seattle, Washington, a truck, some big ideas, and probably a roll of duct tape. By 2010, Michael launched the San Francisco office and took things up a notch. Fast forward to today, and Allied is one of the fastest-growing private companies in America — a fact that surprises no one who’s seen Michael on a mission (or on caffeine). As the fearless leader of Team Allied, Michael is in charge of pointing the ship in the right direction, yelling “let’s go!” a lot, and making sure the culture stays strong, the team stays sharp, and the coffee stays hot. Team Allied Lives by These Fundamentals: 1. Do what’s best for the customer and deliver legendary service. 2. Find a way and go the extra mile (yes, even when it’s uphill). 3. Speak straight — no corporate mumbo jumbo. 4. Create win-win solutions. 5. Celebrate success and keep things fun (bring donuts). 6. Look ahead, anticipate, and be proactive (aka, don’t wait for the roof to leak). 7. Be process-oriented — but not boring. Our Mission: Team Allied is on a mission to make life easier for property managers. Whether you oversee a hotel, school, hospital, church, or government building, if disaster strikes — water, fire, or something else wild — we’ll be there in 60 minutes, 24/7/365, armed with solutions and a can-do attitude. We handle the stress so you don’t have to.
