24/7 Emergency Water Damage Response

When a water emergency strikes, every minute matters. Water migrates into structural cavities, under flooring, and into wall systems at an alarming rate. The difference between a 1-hour and a 3-hour response can mean the difference between saving your hardwood floors and replacing them.

Allied Restoration's emergency response team is always on standby. We maintain fully equipped trucks at our four Bay Area locations — San Francisco, Petaluma, Walnut Creek, and San Jose — so we can reach you within 60 minutes of your call, any time of day or night.

What to Do Right Now (While We're on the Way)

  • Turn off the water source if possible (main shutoff is usually near the water meter)
  • Move valuables, electronics, and important documents to a dry area
  • Do NOT use electrical appliances in flooded areas
  • Take photos of the damage for your insurance claim
  • Do NOT attempt to vacuum up water with a household vacuum
  • Open windows if it's dry outside to help air circulation

Why Speed Matters

Within 1 Hour

Water saturates walls, floors, and contents. Staining and furniture damage begins.

Within 24 Hours

Drywall begins to crack and deteriorate. Metal surfaces begin to rust. Mold begins to germinate.

Within 48–72 Hours

Mold growth accelerates. Structural wood begins to swell. Costs escalate significantly.

After 1 Week

Mold is established. Structural damage is likely. Restoration costs are dramatically higher than if treated promptly.
