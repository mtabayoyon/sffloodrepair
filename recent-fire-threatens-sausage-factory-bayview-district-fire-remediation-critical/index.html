Recent Fire Threatens Sausage Factory in Bayview District, Fire Remediation Critical

It’s no secret that fires can cause serious property damage. A recent story in San Francisco is a good reminder of just how destructive fires can be.

The Evergood Fine Foods store in the Bayview District, a recent site of a major fire, is expected to undergo extensive repairs. The flames caused severe structural damage to the building. It is unclear when the 40-year-old store will resume business as the destruction has rendered it uninhabitable, according to CBS.

Firefighters Struggled to Control the Flames 

Although floods, sewage backups and other property damage can be a nuisance for property owners, fires are an entirely different challenge. Live flames can spread without warning, so a small fire can engulf an entire building within minutes. It is also possible for a fire to reignite itself if the conditions are right. 

At Evergood Fine Foods, firemen remained at the site well into the evening to make sure the flames would remain extinguished. The structural integrity of the site was so badly compromised that many expressed concern over the roof collapsing. Fortunately, no major injuries were reported as a result of the fire.
