Professional Wall Dryout Services

When water penetrates wall assemblies — through a pipe burst, roof leak, or flooding — it gets trapped behind drywall, in insulation, and within wall cavities. This moisture cannot be removed by surface drying alone. Without proper intervention, mold begins growing within 72 hours.

Allied Restoration uses a combination of injectidry systems, wall cavity dryers, and high-velocity air movers to dry wall assemblies from the inside out. We monitor moisture levels daily and report progress to your insurance carrier.

Our Wall Dryout Process

  • Moisture mapping with professional meters and thermal cameras
  • Strategic removal of drywall to access wall cavities when necessary
  • Installation of injectidry panels or wall cavity dryers
  • Daily monitoring with written moisture readings
  • Removal of equipment when dry standard is achieved per IICRC S500
  • Written drying report for your insurance claim
