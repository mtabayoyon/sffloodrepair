Allied Restoration Announces New Award and New Allied Partners Vendor Program

What You Should Know

NOVATO, Calif., (March 28, 2018) — Allied Restoration Company is thrilled to announce that its president, Michael Tabayoyon, was recently recognized as one of North Bay Business Journal’s top 40 under 40 Professionals for 2018. The elite list of young professionals was selected by the journal’s editorial staff out of a total of 191 nominations that were received in January. The winners will be recognized in an awards ceremony on April 20.

As another recent development, Allied Restoration has launched a new Allied Partners Vendor Program (APVP) for large commercial clients and homeowners’ associations. APVP will have a significant impact on clients by giving them top priority to their 24 hour, seven-day-a-week emergency response services. They will also benefit by receiving volume pricing by a local service provider who strives to become a long-term partner in their success. Allied Restoration’s objective is to minimize downtime in an effort to prevent loss of normal business hours of operation and diminish issues with clientele for commercial organizations. “We can respond quickly to emergencies,” said Allied Restoration CEO Michael Tabayoyon. “We are a local company that has the right manpower, tools and expertise to minimize business interruption during catastrophic events.”

Allied Restoration is a full-service restoration company that was founded in the San Francisco Bay area in 1998. Today, it is a multi-generational business that is operated by various members of the Tabayoyon family. The company—which also has offices in Marin County and Sonoma County—is a Licensed General Contractor with a Institute of Inspection, Cleaning and Restoration Certification (IICRC). For 20 years, it has specialized in restoring all types of structures due to water damage, helping to mitigate mold damage in homes as well as contain, remove, clean and sanitize mold. Allied Restoration can work with all major insurance carriers. Once the initial repairs are taken care of, its professional team works with the clients to coordinate additional 24 hour post-repair and clean up options.

Why Professional Help Matters

Having worked with a number of clients over the years, Allied Restoration has a high level of expertise and experience. The company has worked on a variety of projects, including hotels, conference venues, restaurants, commercial buildings, and new buildings that endured water damage while under construction. Allied Restoration is actively building its team of experts, which consists of project managers, emergency technicians, sales professionals, and administrative and financial personnel. “We are a team of professional, smart, driven people that looks forward to building relationships in our community,” Tabayoyon said.

As a trusted organization, Allied Restoration takes immense pride in being a business that is there for the community in times of need. Consequently, the company strives to give back to the local community in and out of crisis circumstances. Allied Restoration is committed to keeping clients’ needs at the forefront and providing diligent, cost-effective solutions for any budget and any situation. “We are driven to do well by doing good,” Tabayoyon said.
