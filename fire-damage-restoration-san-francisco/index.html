Fire & Smoke Damage Restoration in San Francisco

A fire is one of the most devastating events a property owner can face — and what happens in the hours after the flames are out determines how much can be saved. Allied Restoration provides complete fire and smoke damage restoration across San Francisco and the Bay Area, working directly with your insurance from the first assessment through full reconstruction.

Why Speed Matters After a Fire

Smoke and soot are acidic. Within hours they begin etching and corroding surfaces — metal, glass, appliances, and finishes. Within days, that damage can become permanent. Add the water used to extinguish the fire, and a property faces fire, smoke, and water damage simultaneously. The faster professional restoration begins, the more of your home and belongings can be saved. What looks like a total loss is often substantially restorable when work starts quickly.

Our Fire Damage Restoration Process

  • Emergency response & board-up — securing the property and preventing further damage
  • Damage assessment — documenting fire, smoke, soot, and water damage for your insurance
  • Water removal & drying — addressing the water used to fight the fire
  • Soot & smoke removal — cleaning surfaces before acidic residue causes permanent damage
  • Odor neutralization — thermal fogging, hydroxyl, and ozone treatment at the source
  • Contents restoration — saving salvageable belongings
  • Reconstruction — rebuilding to pre-loss condition
