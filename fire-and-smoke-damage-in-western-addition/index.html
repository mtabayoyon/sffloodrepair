Fire and Smoke Damage in Western Addition
Enduring a house fire in your Western Addition neighborhood home can be a devastating experience. You must deal with the loss of your property, possible health risks, and unknown cost to repair and restore your home to its former glory. Many people don't know who to call to start the clean up process when fire and smoke damage has occurred.

After the fire is out, homeowners should relocate to a safe place and call their insurance company. Then, a comprehensive assessment of the damage should be done by a professional from Allied Restoration Co.
