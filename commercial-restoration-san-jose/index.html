Why Equipment Ownership Matters for Large Losses

Drying a large commercial structure fast requires equipment at scale. Many restoration companies rent it only when a big job comes in — adding a sourcing-and-transport delay at exactly the moment speed matters most. Allied owns its large-loss fleet:

  • Desiccant dehumidification — industrial-scale moisture removal for large structures
  • Three-phase temporary power — running high-capacity equipment where building power is down
  • High-volume water extraction — removing large volumes of water fast
  • Air movers and structural drying — deployed at the scale a commercial floor plate requires
