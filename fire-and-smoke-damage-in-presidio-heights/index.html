Fire and Smoke Damage in Presidio Heights
Enduring a house fire in your Presidio Heights neighborhood home can be a devastating experience. There are health risks involved along with the emotional impact of loss of personal property. Afterwards, questions arise about the cost to repair the lingering damage. Most people do not know where to turn after the fire is out.

Your first call after the fire department has put out the fire is to your insurance company. Clean up and restoration will be next. Many people smell smoke residue in their entire home, even in parts that the fire wasn't located. Smoke clings to soft surfaces such as curtains, carpets, clothing, and bedding. Many don't realize that smoke leaves a toxic residue that can be dangerous to you and the internal paint, finishes, and even electrical wiring of your home.
