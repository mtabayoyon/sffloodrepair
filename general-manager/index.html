General Manager

📍 San Francisco Bay Area (4 offices) 💼 Full Time 💰 $110,000 – $140,000 + bonus
Apply Now — dispatch@teamallied.co (415) 529-5637
Allied Restoration Company is seeking an experienced General Manager to lead operations across our four Bay Area offices. You will oversee all aspects of the restoration business — from field operations and team performance to financial reporting and strategic growth. This is a senior leadership role for someone with deep experience in restoration, construction, or a related field who is ready to own P&L responsibility.

Responsibilities

  • Lead and align operations across San Francisco, Petaluma, Walnut Creek, and San Jose offices
  • Own P&L performance — hit revenue, gross margin, and EBITDA targets monthly
  • Hire, develop, and retain field supervisors, project managers, and office staff
  • Ensure IICRC compliance and quality standards on all active projects
  • Manage relationships with insurance carriers, adjusters, and property managers
  • Drive commercial account growth and strategic partnerships
  • Oversee job costing, WIP reporting, and QuickBooks financial review
  • Implement and enforce company SOPs across all departments
  • Report directly to the founder/CEO on KPIs and strategic initiatives

Requirements

  • 5+ years senior management experience in restoration, construction, or property services
  • Track record managing multi-location teams of 20+ people
  • P&L ownership experience with revenue responsibility of $5M+
  • Deep understanding of insurance claims process (Xactimate preferred)
  • Strong systems thinker — experience with CRMs, job management software, and reporting dashboards
  • IICRC certification preferred (WRT, ASD, or equivalent)
  • Excellent communication skills with ability to interface with owners, adjusters, and field crews
  • Valid California driver's license and clean driving record

Compensation & Benefits

  • Base salary $110,000 – $140,000 depending on experience
  • Performance bonus tied to company P&L
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • Paid time off + 10 paid holidays
  • Company vehicle or vehicle allowance
  • IICRC certification support and continuing education
  • Growth path to equity participation for the right candidate
