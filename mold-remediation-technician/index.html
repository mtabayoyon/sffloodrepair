Mold Remediation Technician

📍 San Francisco Bay Area 💼 Full Time 💰 $22 – $32/hr + overtime
Apply — dispatch@teamallied.co (415) 529-5637
Allied Restoration Company is hiring Mold Remediation Technicians for our San Francisco Bay Area operations. You will work on mold containment, physical remediation, and clearance preparation. San Francisco's fog-heavy climate and older Victorian housing stock create consistent year-round demand for mold remediation services.

Responsibilities

  • Set up negative air pressure containment barriers
  • Operate HEPA air scrubbers and negative air machines
  • Perform physical mold removal — HEPA vacuuming and antimicrobial treatment
  • Remove and bag contaminated building materials — drywall, insulation, flooring
  • Apply EPA-registered antimicrobial treatments to all remediated surfaces
  • Set up structural drying equipment after remediation
  • Assist with moisture surveying using Tramex and Delmhorst meters
  • Document all work in Encircle — photos, material quantities, treatment logs
  • Properly dispose of all contaminated materials per regulatory requirements

Requirements

  • IICRC AMRT certification preferred — we will train the right candidate
  • Ability to work in full PPE — Tyvek suits, respirators, gloves
  • Physical ability to work in crawlspaces and confined spaces
  • Valid California driver's license
  • Detail-oriented and methodical work style
  • Background check and drug screening required

Compensation & Benefits

  • $22 – $32/hr depending on certifications
  • Overtime available on large commercial projects
  • Health, dental, and vision after 90 days
  • IICRC AMRT certification paid by company
  • All PPE provided
  • Consistent year-round work — SF climate drives steady mold demand
  • Career path to crew lead and project manager
