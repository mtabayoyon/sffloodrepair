Reconstruction Estimator — Immediate Hire

📍 San Francisco Bay Area 💼 Full Time 💰 $80,000 – $110,000 DOE

Allied Restoration Company is seeking a Reconstruction Estimator for immediate hire. This is an urgent opening for an experienced estimator who can hit the ground running — scoping post-water, fire, and mold reconstruction projects, producing Xactimate estimates, and working directly with insurance carriers to get projects approved and moving. If you have strong Xactimate skills and experience estimating residential and commercial reconstruction, we want to talk to you today.

Responsibilities

  • Scope reconstruction projects following water damage, fire damage, and mold remediation
  • Produce detailed Xactimate reconstruction estimates — Level 2 proficiency required
  • Meet with property owners and insurance adjusters to review and negotiate scope
  • Coordinate with field crews and subcontractors on project specifications
  • Manage multiple active estimate files simultaneously
  • Produce supplemental claims as scope changes are identified during construction
  • Maintain project documentation in company job management system
  • Support project managers through the reconstruction phase

Requirements

  • Xactimate Level 2 certification required — Level 3 preferred
  • 3+ years reconstruction estimating experience in restoration or construction
  • Strong knowledge of residential and commercial construction methods and materials
  • Experience negotiating with insurance adjusters on reconstruction scope
  • Ability to read architectural plans and construction drawings
  • Proficiency with Encircle, JobSight, or similar documentation tools
  • Valid California driver's license
  • Available to start within 2 weeks

Compensation & Benefits

  • Salary $80,000 – $110,000 depending on Xactimate level and experience
  • Performance bonus on closed reconstruction volume
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • Paid time off
  • Xactimate continuing education paid
  • Strong pipeline of active projects — immediate earning potential
  • Fast-growing company with advancement opportunities
