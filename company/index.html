Our Story

15 Years of Restoration Excellence

Allied Restoration Company helps to minimize damage to buildings due to water, fire and mold in San Francisco Ca. We are a full service restoration company specializing in restoration of structures due to water damage. We help to mitigate mold damage in homes as well as contain, and remediate mold.

With our clients' needs in mind we provide diligent, cost-effective solutions to water and mold damage in San Francisco. We continually develop and update our methods to meet new challenges while still satisfying the needs of our clients and all regulatory requirements.
