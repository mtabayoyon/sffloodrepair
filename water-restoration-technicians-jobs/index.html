Water Restoration Technician

📍 San Francisco Bay Area (multiple locations) 💼 Full Time 💰 $22 – $32/hr DOE + overtime
Apply Now — dispatch@teamallied.co (415) 529-5637
Allied Restoration Company is actively hiring Water Restoration Technicians for our San Francisco, Petaluma, Walnut Creek, and San Jose operations. You will respond to water damage emergencies, perform extraction and structural drying, operate LGR dehumidifiers and air movers, and document projects to IICRC standards. This is a hands-on field role with strong earning potential, career advancement, and paid IICRC certification training.

Responsibilities

  • Respond to water damage emergencies — residential and commercial — within our 60-minute response target
  • Perform water extraction using truck-mounted and portable units
  • Set up and monitor structural drying equipment — LGR dehumidifiers, air movers, desiccant units
  • Take and record daily moisture readings using Delmhorst and Tramex meters
  • Document all project activity using Encircle mobile app — photos, logs, readings
  • Install and maintain floor drying systems (Injectidry, floor mat systems)
  • Perform controlled demolition — remove wet drywall, flooring, insulation as needed
  • Maintain and load equipment in company vehicles
  • Participate in on-call emergency rotation

Requirements

  • Ability to perform physically demanding work — lifting up to 75 lbs, working in confined spaces
  • Reliable transportation and valid California driver's license
  • IICRC WRT certification preferred — we will train and certify the right candidate
  • Experience with restoration equipment (Dri-Eaz, Dri-Eaz LGR, Crosswind AX) a plus
  • Strong work ethic and ability to work independently on job sites
  • Available for emergency on-call rotation (evenings and weekends)
  • Bilingual English/Spanish a plus
  • Background check and drug screening required

Compensation & Benefits

  • $22 – $32 per hour depending on IICRC certifications and experience
  • Overtime pay on large-loss projects
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance after 90 days
  • Paid time off after 90 days
  • IICRC WRT, ASD, and AMRT certification paid by company
  • Company uniforms and PPE provided
  • Company vehicle on job sites — no personal vehicle use for work
  • Career advancement to crew lead, project manager, and beyond
