Flood Damage Restoration Across the Bay Area

Flooding is one of the most destructive events a property can face — and one of the most time-sensitive. Whether from a burst pipe, storm, appliance failure, or rising water, flood damage spreads fast and worsens by the hour. Allied Restoration provides complete flood damage restoration across the San Francisco Bay Area: rapid water extraction, structural drying, and full reconstruction, working directly with your insurance from the first call.

Our Flood Damage Restoration Process

  • Emergency response — 24/7, with a 60-minute Bay Area target
  • Water extraction — high-volume pumps and extractors remove standing water fast
  • Structural drying — commercial air movers and dehumidifiers dry the structure to verified standard
  • Moisture mapping — finding hidden water in walls, subfloors, and cavities
  • Sanitizing & decontamination — especially for contaminated floodwater
  • Mold prevention — drying fast enough to stop mold before it starts
  • Reconstruction — restoring the property to pre-loss condition

Why Speed Determines the Outcome

In a flood, every hour matters. Water wicks into drywall, subfloors, and framing; mold begins within 24-48 hours; and contaminated water poses health risks. The faster extraction and drying begin, the less it costs and the more that can be saved. Allied owns its large-loss equipment fleet, so we deploy at scale immediately.
