Commercial fire damage presents unique challenges that residential restoration does not. Business continuity, tenant displacement, code compliance, and commercial insurance documentation all add complexity that requires experienced commercial restoration professionals. Allied Restoration Company has handled commercial fire damage restoration across the San Francisco Bay Area for over 20 years — from small retail store kitchen fires through multi-story commercial building losses.

Commercial Fire Damage Services

Emergency Board-Up & Securing

Commercial board-up of windows, doors, storefronts, and roof openings. Temporary fencing and site security for large commercial losses. Utility disconnection coordination. Immediate response to prevent secondary damage and liability.

Commercial Soot & Smoke Cleanup

Industrial HEPA vacuuming and dry-ice blasting for large surface areas. Commercial HVAC system cleaning and duct decontamination. Specialized cleaning protocols for different commercial materials — steel, aluminum, glass, commercial flooring, and equipment surfaces.

Odor Elimination

Commercial-scale hydroxyl generation and thermal fogging for large commercial spaces. Ozone treatment for unoccupied buildings. Permanent smoke odor elimination verified by air quality testing before occupancy is restored.

Water Damage from Suppression

Sprinkler and firefighting water extraction across all affected areas. Commercial structural drying with desiccant equipment. Concurrent water and fire restoration to minimize total downtime.

Commercial Contents Restoration

Business equipment, inventory, and furniture pack-out, documentation, and restoration. Specialized cleaning for electronics, records, and high-value commercial contents. Complete inventory for commercial property insurance claims.

Commercial Reconstruction

Full commercial rebuild including tenant improvements, ADA compliance updates, commercial-grade finishes, mechanical systems, and San Francisco permit coordination. Single contractor from cleanup through certificate of occupancy.

Minimizing Business Interruption

Every day a commercial property is closed costs money — in lost revenue, ongoing lease obligations, and employee displacement. Allied Restoration prioritizes rapid commercial restoration with:

  • Phased restoration approach — prioritize the areas needed for partial reopening first
  • Extended work hours — crews work evenings and weekends to compress the timeline
  • Dedicated project manager — single point of contact for property manager, insurance adjuster, and tenants
  • Business interruption documentation — we help document the financial impact of the loss for your BI claim
  • Expedited insurance coordination — our Xactimate team works directly with commercial adjusters

Common Commercial Fire Sources in San Francisco

  • Restaurant kitchen fires — San Francisco's dense restaurant corridor sees frequent commercial kitchen fire events
  • Electrical fires — aging commercial building electrical systems in older SF buildings
  • HVAC equipment fires — rooftop mechanical equipment failures
  • Tenant-caused fires — space heaters, candles, and cooking appliances in office spaces
  • Arson — commercial properties in high-traffic urban areas
