Why Commercial Storm Recovery Needs Specialized Capacity

Storm damage to a commercial building is rarely a single-room problem. Water enters through compromised roofs and openings and spreads across large floorplates, into multiple tenant spaces, and through building systems. Recovering this requires the large-loss equipment fleet and crew capacity Allied maintains — desiccant dehumidification for big open spaces, temporary power when the grid is down, and the project management to coordinate it all while keeping stakeholders informed.
