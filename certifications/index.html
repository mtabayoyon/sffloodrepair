IICRC Certified Firm

The Institute of Inspection Cleaning and Restoration Certification (IICRC) is the world's most widely recognized and respected standard-setting body in the inspection, cleaning, and restoration service industry.

💧

WRT — Water Restoration Technician

Technicians certified under IICRC S500 standard for professional water damage restoration, including extraction, drying, and documentation protocols.

🛡️

AMRT — Applied Microbial Remediation

IICRC S520 certified technicians for mold remediation, including containment, HEPA filtration, antimicrobial application, and post-remediation verification testing.

🔥

FSRT — Fire & Smoke Restoration

IICRC S770 certified technicians for fire and smoke damage restoration, including smoke residue removal, odor elimination, and structural cleaning.

🔬

ASD — Applied Structural Drying

Advanced training in psychrometrics and structural drying techniques, including the use of industrial desiccant dehumidifiers and moisture monitoring systems.

🛡️

EPA Lead-Safe Certified

Certified under the EPA Lead-Safe Certification Program, ensuring proper lead-safe work practices in pre-1978 structures that may contain lead-based paint.
