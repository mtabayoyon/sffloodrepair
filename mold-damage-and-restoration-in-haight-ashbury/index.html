Mold Damage and Restoration in Haight Ashbury

Haight-Ashbury Mold Risk: Haight-Ashbury's historic Victorian and Edwardian homes were built in an era before vapor barriers and modern waterproofing. This neighborhood consistently sees high rates of bathroom, kitchen, and basement mold calls.

Mold Damage and Restoration in Haight Ashbury

If you suspect mold in your Haight Ashbury neighborhood home, call Allied Restoration Company today. Mold is notorious for spreading throughout a home with remarkable ease, particularly through HVAC systems in home. Mold spores can travel through the air, landing and growing in other moist areas in kitchens and bathrooms.

Allied Restoration Company will inspect your home by first taking an air sample and then performing a moisture inspection to precisely isolate the area where mold spores may be growing. We will then provide an estimate and create a step-by-step plan to begin an affordable mold remediation procedure. Post-remediation, we will once again inspect the air to confirm all mold spores have been removed from your home. You can then rest assured that your home is safe and the air quality is healthy for you and your family.

If you suspect that your Haight Ashbury neighborhood home has been contaminated by mold, contact Allied Restoration Company today at 415-529-5637 to schedule an appointment at your earliest convenience. One of our experts will be in contact with you to solve this problem quickly and affordably.
