1. Allstate

There are different variables that affect insurance premiums. Allstate allows you to monitor these aspects with their Premium Gauge and smartphone app options. They offer various enhanced features, making them the third ranked insurance company in California.

2. California State Automobile Association

Affiliated with AAA, the CSAA Inter-Insurance Bureau is the fourth largest provider of insurance to Sausalito homeowners. They have a wide array of discounts on offer for multi-policyholders, as well as convenient tools for online quotes and claim management.

In a bid to keep prices down and maintain healthy competition, Fire Insurance Exchange is owned entirely by its policyholders. Over the last decade and a half, they have issued the second highest number of home insurance policies in California.

Possibly the largest automobile insurers in the world, AAA also offers homeowners insurance, with attractive discounts for their members. They are leaders in the insurance industry and there are a variety of insurance options to choose from.
