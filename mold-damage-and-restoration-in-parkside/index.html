Mold Damage and Restoration in Parkside

Parkside Mold Risk: Parkside sits adjacent to Stern Grove and sees consistent fog and moisture from the ocean. Many of the neighborhood's post-war homes have original roofing and minimal attic ventilation, creating ideal conditions for mold.

Finding mold in your Parkside neighborhood home can be unsettling. Black mold can cause serious health issues, especially for those highly sensitive to mold spores. Reactions to the presence of mold can be more dangerous to anyone suffering from asthma, especially in small children or older adults.

Allied Restoration will inspect your home by first taking an air sample and then performing a moisture inspection to precisely isolate the area where mold spores may be growing. We will then provide an estimate and create a step-by-step plan to begin an affordable mold remediation procedure. Post-remediation, we will once again inspect the air to confirm all mold spores have been removed from your home.

If you are looking for a mold remediation service in the Parkside neighborhood area, Allied Restoration Company will provide the best line of defense to remove mold from your home and create a more allergy-free living area for you and your family. Call us today at (415) 529-5637 to begin the mold testing process so we can restore your home from harmful mold.
