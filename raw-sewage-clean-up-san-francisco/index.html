Sewage Clogs in San Francisco

Homeowners in San Francisco are at greater risk for sewage backups. Due to the old, clay sewer lines running under the streets of San Francisco, tree root intrusion and damage from earthquakes are particularly hazardous for plumbing. Heavy rains that flood sewage lines with water and debris are often an issue, and San Francisco's pipe system exacerbates the situation.

Getting in Touch With a Sewage Cleanup Service

Many homeowners make the regretful mistake of trying to solve the situation themselves. This almost always leads to further problems such as contamination and exposure to dangerous pathogens and bacteria. Professional cleaning services understand all the steps to minimize the risk of exposure to the various pathogens, fungal contaminants and viruses that are often present in raw sewage.

Quick Tips While Waiting for Help

Avoid the affected area as much as possible. Do not attempt to clean sewage yourself. Wear rubber boots and a breathing mask if you must be near the area. Contact neighbors as sewage clogs frequently affect more than one home. Call Allied at 415-529-5637 immediately.
