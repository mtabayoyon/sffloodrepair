Mold Damage and Restoration in Presidio Heights

Presidio Heights Mold Risk: Presidio Heights' grand historic homes are among San Francisco's most valuable — and most complex to remediate. Large footprints, multiple bathrooms, and aging pipe systems mean mold issues here tend to be extensive when they occur.

If you suspect mold growth in your Presidio Heights home, call Allied Restoration today. Mold is notorious for spreading throughout a home with remarkable ease, particularly through HVAC systems in home. Mold spores can travel through the air, landing and growing in other moist areas in kitchens and bathrooms.

Allied Restoration will inspect your home by first taking an air sample and then performing a moisture inspection to precisely isolate the area where mold spores may be growing. We will then provide an estimate and create a step-by-step plan to begin an affordable mold remediation procedure. Post-remediation, we will once again inspect the air to confirm all mold spores have been removed from your home. You can then rest assured that your home is safe and the air quality is healthy for you and your family.

If you believe that your Presidio Heights home has been contaminated by mold, contact Allied Restoration today at 415-529-5637 to schedule an appointment at your earliest convenience. One of our experts will be in contact with you to solve this problem quickly and affordably.
