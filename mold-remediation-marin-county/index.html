Mold Remediation in Marin County

Marin County's coastal climate, humidity, and older housing stock create ideal conditions for mold. Allied Restoration provides professional mold remediation across Marin County — safely removing mold, finding and fixing the moisture source, and verifying the air is clean. We handle everything from bathroom mold to whole-structure remediation.

Our Mold Remediation Process

  • Inspection & assessment — finding all mold, including hidden growth
  • Containment — sealing the area to prevent spore spread
  • Moisture source correction — fixing the water problem feeding the mold
  • Safe removal — remediation under proper containment and protection
  • Air filtration — HEPA filtration to clean the air
  • Verification — confirming the remediation is complete

Serving All of Marin County

Allied serves San Rafael, Novato, Mill Valley, San Anselmo, and communities across Marin County. Because mold always has a moisture source, we address the root cause — not just the visible growth. Learn more in our mold remediation field guide or about hidden mold.
