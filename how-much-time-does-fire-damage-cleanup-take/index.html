How Much Time Does Fire Damage Cleanup Take?

When you experience a catastrophe like a house fire, the idea of getting your house back into a livable place can be stressful and scary. However, if you retain a professional fire damage cleanup company for help, they can make the process more efficient and less worrisome overall. Let's look at the steps to getting fire damage fixed and discuss how long the task will take to complete.

How Long the Repairs Will Take
Overall, the amount of time it will take to finish repairing a house or other building damaged by fire will depend on how much damage was done. For situations where there is relatively little damage, it could take a few days for a professional cleanup company to repair, but if the problems are extensive, the task could take six months or even more.

The Steps to the Fire Damage Cleanup Process
Here’s a quick rundown of the steps that need to be taken to get the repairs done:

  • You’ll need to determine how long the repairs will take and how much work needs to be done by getting a professional assessment. A reputable damage control company will be able to give you a detailed overview of what needs to be done and how long it will take.
  • Now it’s time for damage control. A team will come in and remove all the items that can still be salvaged as soon as possible. Leaving them among the debris for too long will make it even harder for them to clean, so it’s important that this is done very early in the process.
  • The cleanup and repair stage is when people will come in to clean all the soot and begin fixing damaged parts of the house. You will also probably want to have your HVAC company repair or replace your air conditioner or vents to get rid of any soot, depending on what your restoration company recommends.
  • Replacing household items that the team was unable to repair or clean up will need to be replaced. This can include things like carpets, appliances, furniture, wallpaper, electrical sockets, and others.
