  2. Turn off electricity in affected areas
  3. Move valuables to dry areas

How to Choose the Best Water Damage Restoration Company Near You

Not all restoration companies are equal. Here's what to verify before you let anyone work on your home:

  • IICRC certification — specifically WRT (Water Restoration Technician) and ASD (Applied Structural Drying). Ask to see the certifications.
  • Local office vs. dispatch center — verify they have an actual office near you, not just a Google listing
  • Xactimate capability — your insurance carrier requires Xactimate documentation. Not all contractors can produce it.
  • References from local projects — ask for references from SF Bay Area projects specifically
  • CA contractor license — verify at cslb.ca.gov. Allied Restoration license #969355.
  • Insurance carrier relationships — ask which carriers they work with regularly
